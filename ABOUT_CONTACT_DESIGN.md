## Design Principles Applied

### Color Consistency
- **Primary**: Deep pink (#8B3A4A) for headings and accents
- **Accent**: Rose gold for icons and highlights
- **Background**: Cream for main sections
- **Text**: Muted foreground for body text

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Headings**: Serif font (Cormorant Garamond), light weight
- **Body**: Sans-serif (Inter), light weight
- **Sizes**: 
  - Hero headings: 6xl-7xl
  - Section headings: 5xl
  - Subsection headings: xl-2xl
  - Body text: base-lg

### Spacing & Layout
- **Generous white space** throughout
- **Consistent padding**: 4-8 units
- **Grid layouts** for organized content
- **Breathing room** between sections

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover effects**: Shadow elevation (300-500ms)
- **Focus states**: Ring effects on form inputs
- **Transitions**: Smooth 300ms transitions
- **Animations**: Fade-in on page load, bounce on scroll

### Accessibility
- **Contrast ratios**: Proper text-to-background contrast
- **Focus states**: Visible ring effects
- **Form labels**: Clear and associated with inputs
- **Icon labels**: Descriptive text accompanying icons

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile-first approach**
- **Breakpoints**: md (768px), lg (1024px)
- **Flexible grids**: Adapt from 1 to 3 columns
- **Touch-friendly**: Adequate button/link sizes
